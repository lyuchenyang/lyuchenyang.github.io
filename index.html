About

I'm currently a PhD student of Natural Language Processing at ML-Labs, Dublin City University supervised by Dr. Jennifer Foster and Dr. Yvette Graham. I received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Northeastern University, China. My research interest is mainly about Natural Language Processing especially on Sentiment Analysis and Question Answering related topics. Among my research experiences, I used to work as a research intern at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab and IBM Research-China. I have the fortune to work with Dr. Jennifer Foster, Dr. Yvette Graham, Prof. Qun Liu, Dr. Lifeng Shang and Dr. Xin Jiang.
